anayvelyse is a poet, translator, and researcher.born 1991, Scorpio  She has written about Estudio Luisita, sisters working in photography on Avenida Corrientes.2019  She interviewed the curators of Radical Women, and with Mafe Izaguirre, she translated Roberto Echeto's "Country Ex-Anima, Venezuela."2018  She has written on public architecture in Buenos Aires2017  and translated "Saturday/February/6/2016" by Sebastián Villar Rojas for The Home Show Book.2017  She is a doctoral candidate in Latin American and Iberian Cultures at Columbia University,since 2014  and union member of Graduate Workers of Columbia.
